    This is a brief description of the games

    Bloodborne
    You play as an unnamed hunter who is in a nightmare of a city called Yharnam where regular people worship a healing blood coming from the gods but gone wrong and turned people into mindless beasts. Your goal is to end your supposed nightmare by facing against what the city has to offer.
    Sekiro
    The game takes place in a mythical world of Feudal Japan in a city called Ashina after a hundred year long war. You play as a nameless shinobi but called by his nickname given by his young lord Kuro “Wolf”. Kuro is special as he has an ancestral curse that makes him immortal and is passed down to his shinobi. The head lord of Ashina Ishin of Ashina is the ruler and the one who ended the hundred year long war but leaves most of the order to his grandson Genichiro and wants to gain the power of the dragon blood heritage to defend the crumbling kingdom of Ashina.
    There is much more to these games. This is only a watered down version of the story behind these games.
